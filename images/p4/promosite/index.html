What's this sweet thing?

Boomereel™ is a collaborative video app for cinematographers that works like no other video collaborating tool currently on the market.

    Why it's so sweet

  • Accessibility
    Made for both beginners and experienced cinematographers
  • Sending
    Collaboration can be between you and your friend, or a random stranger for a unique experience
  • Exploring
    Create, find, and even save your favourite videos for inspiration
