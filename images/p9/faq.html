FAQ

  1. Home
  2. FAQ

When/Where is the event?

Venue gates open at 11:00AM each day, and closed in the event of severe weather.

Head over to our contact page for more information as to where are and where we'll be next!

Will there be hormone- and antibiotic- free selections?

Yes, at Sausagefest we aim to host various ethical vendors which will have hormone and antibiotic free choices for you.

I have several food allergies. Do any of of the sausages contain gluten, soy or dairy products?

Gluten, Soy and dairy are ingredients which many vendors use in their sausages, meatballs and other items. You will need to inquire to the specific vendor about their products for information on allergens.

How can I become a vendor/volunteer?

Please head over to our contact page and fill in the form provided and mention VENDOR or VOLUNTEER in the message, and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Because spaces are limited we cannot guarantee a spot in our festival.

Do you have to purchase admission to enter?

No, admission is free for everyone of all ages!

Will there be parking available?

There will be plenty of parking adjacent to the festival grounds. Day parking is free. Bike space is also provided.

There is no overnight parking allowed.

There is NO parking for RVs.

Lost&Found

There will be a Lost & Found booth which might be clearly marked on the festival guide upon entering, and booth itself will be clearly marked.

Please try to put your contact info in your phone and take a picture of your contact info for your camera in case you lose those things.

You can inquire with lost/found after the festival by using the contact form provided Please describe your lost item in detail.

Are Visa/Mastercard or Debit accepted?

No, however there are ATM machines onsite.

Are pets allowed?

No. There is a City regulation prohibiting pets in the event area during special events due to the large crowds.
