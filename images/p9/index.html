Are you ready to rev up your grill?



39th Anniversary Special

Kicking off Sausagefest's 39th Anniversary with only the finest locally sourced meats cooked using live fire and smoke are on offer to fill your bellies. Cooked by top chefs & eateries this event will incorporate multiple BBQ styles from Diep Thai, Kinara Kitchen’s, Pakastani Grill, and more.

First time?

Taking over the leafy surrounds of Centennial Park, Sausagefest will allow you to smell, taste, discover and learn about all kinds of weiners and wursts, from grilled to smoked, meats and delicacies from local vendors and around the globe, washed down with a choice craft beer, cider and delicious wines. There'll also be demos & workshops from well known chefs, eating challenges, little griller kids area, a grand BBQ cook-off and loads more.
