Sausagefest's 39th anniversary will blow your mind

It's that time of year again as festival and awards season concurrently kick off with what is essentially one of the largest weiner festivals on the globe. Sausagefest this year celebrating its 39th year as one of the festivals where the best vendors showcase their juiciest meats Civilians will have access to a wide range of restaurant and retail specials where the sausage takes centre stage, along with an amazing series of pop-up events showcasing entertainers from all over.

The best sausages in Toronto are reason enough to run out and fill up on propane right away. Meat counters across the city are getting inventive when it comes to stuffings, but that doesn't diminish the deep reverence our butchers have for traditional methods.

We advise coming along by public transport as parking is limited and restricted in the area. DO NOT obstruct driveways, neighbouring properties or park illegally as this can cause great inconvenience to our neighbours. It is also easily accessible by foot, bicycle & car.
