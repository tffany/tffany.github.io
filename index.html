Branding
Project Title

New Student Centre @ YorkU

Logo design and brand development

2015~

This is an ongoing campaign at York University's Keele Campus by York University Student Centre promoting the arrival of the soon-to-be-built New Student Centre. As an identity has yet to be established for the new building, the graphic design team worked alongside the developers of the building to create a visual identity for the campaign. The result is a minimal but bold aesthetic that students can identify with ease when the new building is completed.

A clean and versatile logo that not only can be easily recognizable but give a sense of "home" away from home sets the tone for campaign. Once that was established, many printed material such as promotional posters, banners, and cards as well as infographic boards and pamphlets. In addition to print material, the visual design of the building's website were also established.
