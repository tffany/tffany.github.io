about

a 20-year-old design student who really likes cats.

A student in Toronto's York-Sheridan Joint Program for Design who would like to pursue a career in the field of interactive design, preferring to focus on UI/UX and website design. They also have dabbled in information design, icon development, and branding.

They also have an active interest in illustration and in a little bit of photography. In addition to learning and adapting quickly, they're also eager to experience new things. They're overall a pretty friendly person and definitely sure to be a great addition to the team with their flexibility and problem solving.

Branding
New Student Centre
New Student Centre @ YorkU

This is an ongoing campaign at York University's Keele Campus by York University Student Centre promoting the arrival of the soon-to-be-built New Student Centre. As an identity has yet to be established for the new building, the graphic design team worked alongside the developers of the building to create a visual identity for the campaign. The result is a minimal but bold aesthetic that students can identify with ease when the new building is completed.

   website  |       campaign communications booklet

Campaign/Advertisement
Breaking the Cycle
Breaking the Cycle

A conceptual advertisment campaign for Communications that focuses on promoting the perks of doing an internship, directed at post-secondary young adults. These ads are applied in laundromats, on walls in institutions, and as an applique on a free tape roll to be handed out.

App Development
Boomereel
Ephemeral Messaging

Boomereel is a fun video-based app designed for the iPhone 6+, with a focus on cinematographic roots. Users can sign up with social media and send or 'receive' videos to append to, resulting in a collaborative cinematic video that can then be shared publicly or privately.

   Promotional Site

Collaborators: Simone Robert and Lara Samancioglu.
